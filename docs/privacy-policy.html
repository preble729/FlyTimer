FlyTimer Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 24, 2026

FlyTimer is an Android app for recording sprint timing data, organizing athletes and races, and reviewing results. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

Information FlyTimer Handles

FlyTimer stores information that you enter into the app, such as:

  • Athlete names
  • Race names
  • Recorded times and race history

How Information Is Used

This information is used only to provide the app's core features, including:

  • Saving timing results
  • Displaying athlete and race lists
  • Showing dashboards, recent results, and best times

Data Storage

FlyTimer currently stores app data locally on your device. The app does not currently require an account and does not currently provide its own cloud sync or backend account storage.

Data Sharing

FlyTimer does not sell your personal information and does not intentionally share app data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Analytics And Diagnostics

FlyTimer is configured for Firebase project integration, but the current app build does not rely on Firebase Analytics for core operation. If analytics, crash reporting, or other remote services are added in a future release, this Privacy Policy should be updated before that release is published.

Data Retention And Deletion

Because FlyTimer currently stores data locally on the device, you can remove app data by clearing the app's storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the app, subject to any device-level backup behavior provided by Android or your device manufacturer.

Children's Privacy

FlyTimer is not designed as a service specifically directed to children. If you choose to enter athlete information into the app, you are responsible for making sure you have the right to do so.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.
